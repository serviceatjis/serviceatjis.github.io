Tuesday school

Tuesday School aims to tutor local elementary and middle school students. Every tuesday those students come to JIS to learn English and Math (as well as a few other subjects) from our club members.

Friday School

Friday school not only encourages our group members to interact with local school children in teaching different subjects such as English, Math, Science, and Art, but also gives the JIS club members to develop their teaching skills. In a classroom full of students in ages of grades 1 to 12, club members teach them those basic subjects.

XS Project

XS Project aims to work with children from XS Village (Jakarta’s trashpicker community). Besides the annual Games Day and Health Screening, the children get tutored at JIS every Tuesday in math, english, art and other lessons.

Rumah Indah

Rumah Indah is a service club that works with local Indonesian students, helping them in their studies. We help them with their English by giving them grammatical english lessons and then with their requests we help them with work from their other school classes.

Puspita Project

Puspita Community Project is a service club that aims to help and teach the children of the Puspita Community. We help them learn English, Geography, Math and many more hoping that they can fulfill their future dreams and be happy.

Take a Bow

Take a Bow is a service club that teachers local children the basics of English through theatre. Our main focus during meetings is running theatre games that the kids will not only enjoy, but also gain some knowledge from.

Luarbiasa

Luarbiasa is a service club that aims to help and educate young children. We visit orphanage ‘Kasih Mandiri’ and do creative activities with them and teach them art crafts.

Medical Missions

The Medical Missions service club aims to promote physical health and wellbeing in children from local communities. We give the children access to medical care by arranging semi-annual checkups that the entire high school community is encouraged to participate in; we also hold weekly tuesday sessions to educate the kids on healthy lifestyle choices.

KDM

KDM club is a service club that teaches elementary and middle school students the basics of different types of science. We work with a group of children from the KDM community, and help them expand their scientific knowledge, and also answer ahy of their questions. Our club is filled with various fun activities, and interesting scientific labs. In addition, the students are weekly quizzed and often given homework (based on what is taught in class).

DS kids

DS Kids is a fun service club that interacts with children who have Down Syndrome, from Sekolah Insan Anugerah. We work together and carry out exciting games and activities with the children every Tuesday and our goal is to help develop interaction and relationships between the members and the children hoping they enjoy and learn projects that encourage creativity and sociability.

DIY club

Do It Yourself (DIY) club incorporates creativity into community social service. It focuses in teaching kids to create useful and/or educational materials from other recyclable/reusable materials, as well as focusing on teaching young Indonesian children from the local schools the importance of the 3R’s: Reduce, Reuse, Recycle.

Service Climbing Club

Service climbing club interacts with children from local communities through wall climbing. It aims to teach children how to climb, but also to strengthen the bond between JIS students and children.

Popular Musicians Union

PMU’s aim is to provide a medium of communication and collaboration for the musicians of JIS, and primarily oversees the organization of two large concerts throughout the year, JIStock and Unplugged. We divert some amount of the proceeds of the concerts to other service clubs/humanitarian organizations of our choosing.

Art Service Club

Art Service Club promotes artistic exploration within the local community, especially for students who don’t have visual arts as one of their subjects in their academic curriculum. ASC enhances their artistic experiences through creative art projects and enriching their lives with art.

Tri-M

The Tri-M® Music Honor Society is an international program dedicated to the recognition of exceptional music students in grades 6–12 that meet the music, academic, leadership, and character criteria asked of every Tri-M member. Tri-M is a program of MENC: The National Association for Music Education, which is the largest arts education association in the world.

Engineering Off-Grid

Engineering off-Grid is a brand new club which aims to help those in need, especially Indonesians living without access to electricity and clean water. We use creativity and human ingenuity to solve pressing issues in our society, and have fun doing it.

Conquer Cancer Club

Conquer Cancer Club have fundraising projects to donate money to cancer fighting organization such as YKAKI and Mary’s Cancer Kiddies. Our club also organize visits to Y'Kaki, a house where indigent children with cancer live.

Plugged-In

At Plugged In, we strive to enhance the lives of children by educating them with the basic elements of digital literacy. Children are taught programs such as Microsoft Word, Powerpoint, iMovie, etc. and have the opportunity to use these forms of technology to create unique and fun projects which are the foundations of lifelong learning. We also focus on fundraising so that we can provide these children with the resources and tools that they need in order to prosper for the long term.

Habitat For Humanity

Habitat for Humanity (H4H) aims to build decent housing for those in need. Led by the H4H Student Leadership Team and their faculty advisor, students raise enough to finance eight houses and build 12 houses for families in an area above Sentul City.

Rawamangun Club

Rawamangun Club works towards strengthening the bond between JIS and the Rawamangun village by interacting with the children part of the community. Our objective and ambition as a service club is to contribute towards the growth of the children's knowledge while taking into consideration the development of their creativity and inquisitiveness.

Global Issues Network (GIN)

The GIN club is a service club that not only organizes the annual GINDO conference with many different international schools around Jakarta, but also tries to raise awareness for different service issues--both environmental and humanitarian.

Womenesia

Womenesia is a pro feminism club that believes in the power of female individuals. We believe that females are beautiful human beings and that deserve to be treated as such.

Big Siblings

Big Siblings focuses on cultivating a personal relationship with the elderly at the Amal Mulia orphanage through weekly interaction.
